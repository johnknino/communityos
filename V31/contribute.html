Carry the Memories Forward

This platform was built by one person. It should not depend on one person. Here's how you can help.

Every tool on this platform follows three questions: How does this work? Where does it fail? What can you do? If you can answer those questions about anything — health, money, rights, food, housing — you can contribute.

🌐 Translate a Page

The platform is bilingual (English + Spanish). Adding a third language means communities that can't use it today can use it tomorrow. Hindi alone reaches 600 million people.

How it works:

Every page has a translation block inside the code. It looks like pairs of labels and text — one in English, one in Spanish. To add a new language, you copy the English block, rename it to your language code, and translate each line. No programming needed. Just text.

Example:
English: title: 'Community Discussion'
Spanish: title: 'Discusión Comunitaria'
Hindi: title: 'सामुदायिक चर्चा'

⚠ If you translate health or legal content, please have a professional in that field review your translation. Medical mistranslation can harm people. Legal mistranslation can cost people their rights.

⏱ Time: 30-60 minutes per page. No code skills required.

📘 Write a Knowledge Guide

Knowledge guides explain systems that affect your community. Each guide answers three questions about one system.

The structure:

Normal — How does this system work when everything goes right? (2-4 sentences)

Broken — Where does it fail? What catches people off guard? (2-4 sentences)

Fix — What can you do? Specific steps, phone numbers, exact words to say. (2-4 sentences)

Action: One sentence. The single most important thing to do right now.

What makes a good guide:

• Written from experience, not from Google
• Specific to a real situation someone actually faced
• Includes at least one phone number or concrete next step
• Doesn't assume the reader has insurance, a car, English fluency, or internet at home

⏱ Time: 20-30 minutes. Submit through the Knowledge page or email.

🏘️ Start a Community

Any neighborhood can join. Your community owns its own data from day one.

What you need:

1. A Google account (personal or Workspace)
2. 15 minutes to set up a Google Sheet from the template
3. A zip code
4. One person willing to be the Data Steward

What's a Data Steward? A trusted community member who maintains the Sheet. Reviews flagged content. Verifies resource accuracy. Updates local information. Not an authority — a custodian selected by the community.

Cost: $0. Every Google Sheet, every Apps Script deployment, every page — free. The community never pays for anything.

⏱ Time: 15 minutes for setup. Then it's yours.

✅ Verify Content

The most valuable contribution might be the simplest: check whether the information is still accurate. Is the food pantry phone number still working? Did the SNAP threshold change? Is the legal aid hotline still staffed in Spanish? One wrong phone number at 11pm can mean someone doesn't get help.

⏱ Time: 10 minutes. Call a number. Report what you find.

💻 Review the Code

The entire codebase is vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No React. No npm. No build tools. Right-click any page, View Source, and you can read it.

Why no frameworks:

Because a community member can View Source and understand what the page does. Because static HTML works on a $30 phone on 3G. Because there's no build step, no dependency chain, no node_modules. Because Netlify serves it for free forever.

If you improve something, test:
• On a phone (most users are mobile)
• In Spanish (toggle the ES button)
• Offline (turn off WiFi, reload)
• With a screen reader (TalkBack on Android, VoiceOver on iOS)

What Not to Contribute

• Tracking, analytics, or any code that identifies individual users
• Features that require an account, email, or login
• Dependencies on paid services
• Content that is partisan or advocates for a specific candidate
• AI-generated translations without human review
• Any content that could endanger undocumented community members
