Issues & Candidates

Democracy isn't one question with one answer. It's many questions, each requiring its own lens. Evaluate candidates on where they stand — then track whether they do what they said.

📋 Record a Candidate's Position

When a candidate takes a public position on an issue — at a town hall, in a debate, on their website — record it here. The community builds the most complete picture of where every candidate stands.

Positions are community-documented. Sources are required. The Bayesian scoring engine weights recent evaluations more heavily and requires 5+ independent submissions before displaying scores. No identifying data collected.
