Your First Two Weeks

Week 1: Explore and Share
Enter your zip code. Browse the guides. Try the benefits screener. Look up your officials. Share one guide with someone who needs it today.
Week 2: Activate Your Circle
Redeem your code at onboard.html. You'll get civic tokens and 3 invite codes. Give them to three people you trust. Post the first discussion. The community starts with you.
Week 3+: The Platform Learns
Activity grows. The diagnostic engine watches for patterns. Within a month: a civic health report you can hand to your alderman or attach to a grant.

What This Is Not

Not social media. No likes, no followers, no algorithm. This is civic infrastructure.

Not surveillance. No accounts, no logins, no data collection. In neighborhoods where ICE enforcement is real, this matters.

Not a startup. $7/year. Open source. No one profits from your participation.
